Good to Know

Questions, answered.

When does work actually start on my project?+
Once we've agreed on scope and received the initial deposit, we begin discovery and design right away — most projects kick off within days.
How is pricing determined, and how does payment work?+
Each service has a starting price covering the standard build. Your final number depends on pages, features, and complexity, and we walk you through it before anything is confirmed. Payment is typically split between an upfront deposit and completion.
Why are custom systems quoted separately?+
Software like CRMs, POS, or internal dashboards varies enormously in scope. We quote these individually so you only pay for what your business actually needs.
Is SEO really included, or is that extra?+
It's included. Every site we launch ships with core SEO groundwork already in place — no upsell required.
What do I actually get with a maintenance plan?+
Updates, security monitoring, bug fixes, performance improvements, content edits, and direct support — so your site stays reliable without you having to think about it.
Who owns the website once it's finished?+
You do. Once the project is complete and paid in full, the site and its content belong to you.
